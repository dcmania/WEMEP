Wind Conditions 
===============

The IEA-Wind Task 31 "Wakebench" is rolling out the validation strategy for wind assessment models originating from the New European Wind Atlas (`NEWA <http://www.neweuropeanwindatlas.eu/>`_) project in compliance with the Wind Energy Model Evaluation Protocol (MEP). The validation strategy address the needs of the following wind energy applications:

* Wind resource assessment  
* Wind turbine site suitability
* Numerical site calibration
