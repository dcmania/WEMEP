Community Sponsors
==================

WEMEP is primarily developed under the umbrella of the `IEA-Wind <https://community.ieawind.org/home>`_ based on the following research Tasks:

* `IEA Task 31 "Wakebench" <https://windbench.net/wakebench2>`_: modeling external wind conditions and wind farm wakes
* IEA Task XX

with support from the following research projects:

* `New European Wind Atlas (NEWA) <http://www.neweuropeanwindatlas.eu/>`_
* `Atmosphere to Electrons (A2e) <https://www.energy.gov/eere/wind/atmosphere-electrons>`
* etc


with institutional support from the following organizations:
 
* `National Renewable Energy Centre (CENER) <http://www.cener.com/>`_, Spain
* `National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) <http://www.nrel.gov/>`_, USA
* `Sandia National Laboratories (SNL) <http://www.sandia.gov/>`_, USA
* etc
